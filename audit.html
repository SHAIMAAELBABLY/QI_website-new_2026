Audit Services

Independent Assessments for Stronger Systems

At Quality Institution for Engineering and Management Consultancy (QI), we provide professional auditing services designed to help organizations evaluate their systems, improve performance, and ensure compliance with international standards and best practices. Our audits support organizations in identifying gaps, enhancing operational efficiency, and achieving continual improvement.

1st Party Audit

First-party audits are internal assessments conducted by or on behalf of the organization to evaluate the effectiveness of its management systems and internal processes

2nd Party Audit

Second-party audits are conducted to evaluate suppliers, contractors, or business partners to ensure they meet the required standards, contractual obligations, and performance expectations

3rd Party Audit

Third-party audits are independent evaluations conducted by qualified auditors to assess an organization’s compliance with international standards or specific regulatory and contractual requirements.
