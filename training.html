IRCA Accredited Courses

We offer internationally recognized training programs accredited by IRCA, one of the most respected certification bodies for management system auditors worldwide.

These courses are designed for professionals seeking to become certified auditors or enhance their auditing skills in accordance with international standards such as ISO.

Key Benefits:

  • Globally recognized certification
  • Qualification for auditor roles (Lead Auditor / Internal Auditor)
  • Practical, real-world auditing skills
  • Compliance with international ISO standards

ASC Accredited Courses

QI also delivers specialized training programs accredited by the American Standardization Council (ASC), focusing on professional development across various fields.

These courses are ideal for individuals and organizations aiming to improve performance, build competencies, and meet international best practices.

Key Benefits:

  • Internationally accredited certification
  • Flexible training for individuals and companies
  • Focus on practical skills and career development
  • Wide range of professional fields
